A Canvas for the Senses

Our lounge is more than a bar; it's a living gallery. Each month, we curate a selection of contemporary Indian art that lines our charcoal-washed walls. Sip on botanical infusions while surrounded by masterpieces that challenge and inspire.

From the hand-carved teak accents to the custom-designed brass fixtures, every element of the Sukhi Lounge is a testament to Modern Heritage.
